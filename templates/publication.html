The Role of Cloud Computing in Scaling Startups: Balancing Technology and Business Growth

This paper examines how startups can leverage cloud computing effectively by balancing its benefits and challenges. Through analysis of both successful and unsuccessful cases, including Zoom, Slack, and Dropbox—it explores cost structures, security concerns, and organizational impacts.

Arjun Santhosh (First Author) ,Co-Authors ( Anu Tiji , Devika B Menon , Haripriya S , Naithan Thomas )
International Research Journal on Advanced Engineering and Management (IRJAEM) 2(12):3656-3664
